About

I reworked a music recommendation system I made back in 2022 using the Spotify API based on a user's top tracks within the last 4 weeks. Users can (and quite frankly, most likely will) get song recommendations from all over the world.

How It Works

After getting a user's top tracks, I used this dataset from Kaggle to recommend songs by using KMeans to recommend songs within the songs from the largest occuring cluster in a user's top tracks and by using the Euclidean distance to find tracks closest to the weighted average of a user's top tracks. I also multiplied the two metrics to achieve a combined result. As language was not used to train the models, users may be recommended songs from other languages.

Users can also listen to previews of the songs (except for those that do not have preview urls) and create a playlist with those 15 songs if they'd like.
